Font
Highlight the area and choose fonts in Combo Box. Press the font that you would like to use
Size
Highlight the area and choose the size of texts in Combo Box. Press the size that you would like to use.
Bold
To make your text bold, highlight the area and press Bold. If you want to disable the function press its button again.
Italic
To make your text italics, highlight the area and press Italics. If you want to disable the function, press its button again.
Underline
To make your text underlined, highlight the area and press underline. If you want to disable the function,
press its button again.
ForeColor
To put colors in your text, highlight the area and press color drop down button.
Then, choose the color that you would like to change.
BackColor
To put background colors in your text, highlight the area and press background color dropdown button.
Then, choose the color that you would like to change.
Justify Left
To align your text at the left side of the editing area, highlight the area you want to be aligned and press Left Alignment.
Justify Center
To align your text at the center of the editing area, highlight the area you want to be aligned and press Center Alignment.
Justify Right
To align your text at the right side of the editing area, highlight the area you want to be aligned and press
Right Alignment.
OrderList
To create numbered list, highlight the lines you want to be numbered. Then, the text will automatically gain the number
one on the list. If you want to stop Numbered listing, just press again. The function will deactivate.
UnorderList
To create bulleted list, highlight the lines you want to be bulleted. Then, the text will automatically gain the bullets
on the list. If you want to stop Bulleted listing, just press again. The function will deactivate.
Letter Spacing
To put Letter-Spacing in your text, highlight the area and press Letter-Spacing drop down button.
Then, choose the one that you would like to change.
Line Height
To put Line-Height in your text, highlight the area and press Line-Height drop down button.
Then, choose the one that you would like to change.
Strike
To put strike lines on your font, highlight the area and press Strike lines. If you want to disable the function press
its button again.
Sub Script
Highlight the area you want to put subscript, press Subscript. Then, the text will be placed the bottom of index.
If you want to disable the function press its button again.
Super Script
Highlight the area you want to put superscript, press Superscript. Then, the text will be placed the top of index.
If you want to disable the function press its button again.
Outdent
Highlight the area and press Increase indentation button. The very front of text line will have no space.
Indent
Highlight the area and press Decrease indentation button. The front of text line will have space.
